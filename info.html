Gardening:

Gardening is a lifeskill that allows players to level up by creating and sculpting nature! Create lovely gardens, grow trees and decorate your home with beautiful plants. The more you garden, the more you will level up your Gardening Lifeskill, which will allow you to purchase special items from the market.

Ways to Level:

  • Growing Trees with Bonemeal
  • Breaking Grass in your Garden

Gardens:

Garden Markers are special blocks that grow grass in a 10x10 area while you are nearby! This grass can be used to level up your Gardening Lifeskill. Garden Markers can be obtained from the market, but require you to be at least level 2 or above in Gardening to purchase.

Cooking:

Cooking is a big part of the server. With so many custom ingredients and meals, you will be busy chef'ing it up for hours! Recipes range from super simple to moderately complex. The more complicated the meal, the more it will be worth when you sell them! The more you cook, the more you will level up your Cooking Lifeskill, which will allow you to purchase special items from the market.

Ways to Level:

  • Crafting ingredients and meals
  • Cooking ingredients and meals

Market:

The market is a place where you can purchase special ingredients and items that are not typically found in the world. You can access the market by warping there using the command "warp market".

Decorating:

Decorating is a fun way to express your creativity and make your home feel cozy! With a wide variety of furniture and decorations, you can create a unique space that reflects your personality. The more you decorate, the more you will level up your Decorating Lifeskill, which will allow you to purchase special items from the market.

Ways to Level:

  • Crafting tools and materials
  • Crafting furniture

Market:

Much like with Cooking, the market is a place where you can purchase special items that are not typically found in the world. You can access the market by warping there using the command "warp market". A merchant, found only there, will sell you special items that can be used to craft even more unique furniture and decorations.

Farming:

Farming is a lifeskill that allows players to grow and harvest crops, which can be used in cooking or sold for profit. As with many aspects of CottageCraft, there are many custom crops and items that can be grown, making farming feel fresh and exciting! The more you farm, the more you will level up your Farming Lifeskill, which will allow you to purchase special items from the market.

Ways to Level:

  • Growing and harvesting crops
  • Composting excess seeds

Market:

Similarly to Cooking and Decorating, the market is a place where you can purchase special items that are not typically found in the world. You can access the market by warping there using the command "warp market".

Fishing:

Fishing is a lifeskill that allows players to catch fish in various bodies of water. With many custom fish, you can spend hours trying to catch them all! The more you fish, the more you will level up your Fishing Lifeskill. Unlike other lifeskills, fishing does not directly benefit from the market, but instead has a unique prestige system that allows you to reset your fishing level in exchange for special items!

Ways to Level:

  • Catching fish!

Collection:

As you fish, you will collect various types and rarities of fish. These fish can be sold using the "sellfish" command, or can be kept as a trophy in your home. As you catch new fish, you will fill out your Fish Collection, which can be viewed using the "fish collection" command. Once you complete your collection, you will receive a special reward that can be used to purchase unique fishing gear and items from the market.

Prestige:

Once reaching level 30 in Fishing, you can choose to prestige your fishing level via the "prestige" command. This will reset your fishing level back to 1, and you will lose all of your fish collection progress. However, you will gain a special item that can be used to purchase unique fishing gear and items from the market.
